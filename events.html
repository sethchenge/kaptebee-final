Annual Science Fair
Event Details
  • Date: May 25, 2025
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Location: School Auditorium
  • Contact: Mr. Ng'etich (Director of Innovation)
About This Event

The Annual Science Fair is our flagship event where students showcase their innovative STEM projects. This year's theme is "Sustainable Solutions for a Better Future," focusing on how technology can address environmental challenges.

The event will feature:

  • Project exhibitions from all grade levels
  • Guest speakers from leading technology companies
  • Hands-on workshops for visitors
  • Awards ceremony for outstanding projects

Parents, community members, and representatives from local universities and businesses are invited to attend and interact with our young scientists.

Robotics Competition
Event Details
  • Date: June 10, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
  • Location: Innovation Hub
  • Contact: Mr. Magoi (Head of School Department)
About This Event

Our robotics team will be competing in the Regional Robotics Championship, where schools from across the region showcase their robot designs and programming skills.

This year's challenge involves designing robots that can:

  • Navigate complex obstacle courses
  • Perform automated sorting tasks
  • Solve real-world problems through engineering solutions

Come support our team as they demonstrate months of hard work and innovation!

Entrepreneurship Day
Event Details
  • Date: June 18, 2025
  • Time: 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Location: Multi-purpose Hall
  • Contact: Prof. Denis Kisembe (Deputy Principal)
About This Event

Entrepreneurship Day is an exciting opportunity for our students to present their business ideas and startup projects to a panel of local entrepreneurs, investors, and business mentors.

The event will include:

  • Student business plan presentations
  • Product and service demonstrations
  • Networking opportunities with business professionals
  • Seed funding competition for the most promising ventures

We invite parents, local business owners, and community members to attend and support our young entrepreneurs as they take their first steps into the business world.
