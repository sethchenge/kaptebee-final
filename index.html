Meet Our Leadership

Dr. Josiah Korir

Senior Principal

With over 20 years of experience in education, Dr. Korir leads our institution with a vision for innovation and excellence in Kenyan education.

"Our goal is to create future leaders who can harness technology to address global challenges."

Mr. Denis Kisembe

Deputy Principal

Mr. Denis Kisembe leads curriculum development with a focus on integrating theoretical knowledge into practical skills that enables students to tackle real life situations.

"Education should bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world problem-solving."

Mr. Jonathan Magoi

Head of Agriculture Department

With a background in agriculture and biology, Mr. Magoi leads our institution by initiating and overseeing the implementation of theoretical knowledge to farm practicals.

"The future belongs to those who can adapt and innovate in a rapidly changing technological landscape."

Mr. Duke Omwana

Director of Studies

Mr. Duke ensures that our educational environment supports the holistic development of every student, balancing academic rigor with social and emotional growth.

"We aim to nurture not just brilliant minds, but compassionate leaders."

Mr. Ng'etich

Welfare Senior Master

Mr. Ng'etich leads our institution, facilitating partnerships with departmental leaders and creating opportunities for students to engage with emerging ideas.

"Innovation occurs at the intersection of disciplines, where diverse perspectives come together."
