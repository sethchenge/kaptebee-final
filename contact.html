Frequently Asked Questions

Parents and visitors are welcome to visit our campus Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. For tours or meetings with administration, we recommend scheduling an appointment through our main office.

To apply for admission, you can download the application form from our website or pick one up from our admissions office. Complete applications should be submitted along with required documents to our admissions department. For more details, please contact our admissions office directly.

Parents can get involved through our Parent-Teacher Association (PTA), volunteering for school events, participating in parent workshops, or supporting school projects. We regularly communicate opportunities for involvement through our newsletter and parent portal.

Yes, we offer transportation services for students within designated areas. Bus routes, schedules, and fees can be obtained from our transportation department. For specific inquiries about transportation, please contact our office directly.
